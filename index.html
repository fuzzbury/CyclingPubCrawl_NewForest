Tips & Practical Notes

🍺 Pacing8 pubs over the day — 1 pint per stop max to stay safe on the bike. Eat a proper meal at stop 4 (Royal Oak) and/or stop 6 (Horse & Groom).
⚠️ Royal OakClosed Mon & Tue — fine for Fri 11th or Sat 12th. Only serves ploughman's — perfect for a mid-ride lunch at mile 15.
⚠️ Landford PoacherClosed Mon & Tue — open from noon on both your dates. Arrive by 12:30pm on this early leg.
📍 NavigationDownload the route to Komoot or Strava before you go. OS Explorer Map OL22 covers the area. No signal in parts of the forest.
🚲 Bike hireNew Forest Cycling in Burley or Cyclexperience in Brockenhurst if anyone needs to hire.
🌡️ July conditionsExpect 20–25°C. Carry at least 750ml water — the Fritham–Frogham stretch is exposed heathland with no shade.
☀️ DaylightSunrise ~5am, sunset ~9:15pm. Starting at 10am gives you over 11 hours of daylight.
